About Crystal Heart:

I have played video games for as long as I can remember. Growing up, it was the thing I looked forward to most after school, and now it is my go to activity when I need to wind down after a long day. Crystal Heart is a site where I can review some of my favorite games of all time and talk about what makes them so great and why I grew to love them. The name "Crystal Heart" pays homage to two of my favorite game series: Final Fantasy and Kingdom Hearts.

My Review Process is as Follows:

  • Gameplay
  • Sound and Music
  • Story
  • Artstyle
