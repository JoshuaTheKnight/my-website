Nintendo is One of My Favorite Game Companies

Growing up, my fondest memories of playing games are closely tied to Nintendo. Whether it was trying to beat the final level of New Super Mario Bros or hiding under my bedsheets past my bedtime to play Pokémon, Nintendo games were a significant part of my childhood and I still enjoy them to this day. In fact, Nintendo games have had such an impact on me that I want to take the time to write about my favorite titles and what makes them so great.

My Review Process is as Follows:

  • Gameplay
  • Sound and Music
  • Story
  • Artstyle
