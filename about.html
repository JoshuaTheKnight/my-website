About the Author

Having been captivated by video games from an early age, I developed a deep appreciation for their immersive storytelling, breathtaking visuals, and engrossing gameplay. Fond memories of my childhood revolve around navigating Spyro's enchanting world on the PlayStation 2 or indulging in clandestine Pok√©mon sessions, hiding under the bedsheets to play for an extra hour before I went to bed. These experiences have instilled in me an enduring love for the medium.

Motivated by my profound admiration for video games, I endeavored to establish a platform that deviates from the prevailing tide of negativity often associated with critical evaluations.
